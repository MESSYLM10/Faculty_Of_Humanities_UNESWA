History Department

Exploring African and world history to understand past, present, and future societal developments.

About the Department

The History Department at the University of Eswatini is dedicated to understanding the complexities of human societies through time. We specialize in African history while maintaining a global perspective, examining how historical forces have shaped contemporary society.

Our department emphasizes critical analysis, research methodologies, and the development of historical consciousness. We prepare students to become informed citizens, educators, researchers, and professionals who can contribute meaningfully to society's understanding of its past and future direction.

Mission

To foster critical understanding of historical processes, with particular emphasis on African history, while developing students' analytical and research skills for academic and professional success.

Vision

To be the leading center for historical research and education in Southern Africa, recognized for excellence in African historical scholarship and innovative teaching methods.
