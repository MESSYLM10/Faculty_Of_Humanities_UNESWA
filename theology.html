Theology and Religious Studies

Interfaith dialogue and theological scholarship promoting understanding across religious traditions.

About the Department

The Department of Theology and Religious Studies provides a comprehensive and inclusive approach to understanding religious traditions, spiritual practices, and theological discourse. We foster critical thinking about religious beliefs while promoting interfaith understanding and dialogue.

Our department recognizes the central role of religion in human culture and society, particularly in the African context. We prepare students for careers in ministry, counseling, education, community development, and interfaith relations while encouraging scholarly research in theological and religious studies.

Mission

To provide excellent education in theology and religious studies that promotes interfaith understanding, critical thinking, and spiritual development while serving the religious and spiritual needs of our communities.

Vision

To be the leading center for theological education and religious studies in Southern Africa, recognized for fostering interfaith dialogue, spiritual development, and scholarly excellence.

Core Areas of Study

  • Biblical Studies and Hermeneutics
  • Systematic Theology
  • Comparative Religious Studies
  • African Traditional Religion
  • Christian Ethics and Moral Theology
  • Interfaith Dialogue and Relations
  • Pastoral Care and Counseling
  • Philosophy of Religion
  • Religious History and Sociology

Interfaith Initiative

Our department actively promotes understanding between different religious traditions through dialogue programs, joint research projects, and community engagement activities. We believe that mutual understanding and respect among different faiths contributes to peaceful coexistence and social harmony.
