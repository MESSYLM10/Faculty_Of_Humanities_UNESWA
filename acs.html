Academic Communication Skills

Developing essential communication competencies for academic and professional success across all disciplines.

About the Department

The Department of Academic Communication Skills serves as the cornerstone of effective communication across the University of Eswatini. We are dedicated to developing students' abilities in written, oral, and digital communication essential for academic excellence and professional success.

Our comprehensive programs focus on critical thinking, analytical writing, presentation skills, and cross-cultural communication competencies that prepare graduates for the globalized workplace.

Mission

To enhance students' communication abilities and foster critical thinking skills necessary for academic achievement and professional excellence in an increasingly interconnected world.

Vision

To be the leading department in Southern Africa for academic communication training, recognized for innovative pedagogy and excellence in preparing students for global citizenship.
